🛡️ Safe Outreach Rules

Daily checklist to maintain high account reputation and response rate.

✕
Do not message weak leads: Focus outreach only on priority A and B. Weak leads waste limits and decrease response ratios.
⚠️
No multi-channel flooding: Do not contact the same lead on Instagram, LinkedIn, and WhatsApp on the same day. Space touches by 4-5 days.
✓
Email first: Prioritize business email when publicly available. It is the most professional channel for corporate contracts and agencies.
⚠️
WhatsApp restriction: Contact on WhatsApp only if they publicly showcase their number as a business support or sales path.
✓
Follow up max 2 times: Send a maximum of 1 or 2 friendly nudges. If they remain silent, change status to No Reply and move on.
✕
No AI jargon: Do not mention "AI", "ChatGPT", or automated writing in your first pitches. Clients pay for bespoke, high-quality human expertise.
✓
Archive inactive: Switch cold, uninterested, or silent leads to Archived or Not Now to keep the dashboard clean.

⭐ Priority Tier System

A Strong lead: Contact first. Fit is strong, active, need signal.
B Good relevant lead: Need signal is less clear.
C Weak lead: Or maybe later.
D Bad fit: Not relevant, or archive.
